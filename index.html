Budget Travel Tips

Travel more, spend less — smart tips for every explorer

🗓️

Book in Advance

Flights and hotels booked 6–8 weeks early can save up to 40% on costs.

🚂

Travel by Train

Indian Railways connects almost everywhere — affordable, scenic, and reliable.

🏠

Stay in Hostels

Hostels and homestays often offer the best local experience at a fraction of the cost.

🍛

Eat Local

Street food and local dhabas are not just budget-friendly — they are the real taste of India.
